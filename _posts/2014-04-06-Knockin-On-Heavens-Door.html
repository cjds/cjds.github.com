Knockin' On Heavens Door

-Bob Dylan

Inspired by the A Lyric A Day project by Luke Beard. I plan to do one song in pictures each week.

Knockin' On Heavens Door is one of Dylan's most famous songs. This 12 line epic tells a story of a sheriff dying from a bullet wound. One of its most notable covers is by Guns 'N' Roses. [YouTube]
